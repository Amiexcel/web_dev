Times and Seasons

Being sensitive to times and seasons
is one of the ways we beautify our character
and interactions with our fellow humans.

While you're in seasons of celebration,
another might be in season of mourning
or sadness and vice versa.

Sometimes we are caught up in our
small perfect world and we become oblivious
of what another might be going through,
hence we're more concerned with keeping records
of people that showed up to celebrate with us
and we build up an illusion that those that didn't
show up are "haters"

Have you ever in your small world of
insensitive paranoia checked up on people to even know
why they did not rise to the occassion?
Do not be delusional...

Self Worth

The only one who gets to decide your worth is you.
It doesn't come from your bank account
or the number of friends you have.
It's called seld worth for a reason
because it comes from you.

It comes from being yourself
and being proud of who you are.
It comes from being someone that you can count on
and someone you love.
The numbers will change with time,
but what won't change is who you are deep inside.

You are beautiful, limitless,
wonderful, creative, strong, capable...
and that is where your worth comes from
